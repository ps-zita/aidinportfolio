Hey, I'm Aidin Murtha

I'm an agender, gen Z voice actor with a wide range, a unique sense of humor, and a distinct, warm speaking voice. I have a passion for all kinds of voice acting, but have been deeply obsessed with animation of all kinds since childhood and am eager to be able to bring my talents to that world! I would love nothing more than to help you have your creation realized with the level of respect, sincerity, and passion that it deserves - don't hesitate to reach out!

I also am a hyper-niche internet microcelebrity, where my comedic anime dubs have become a surreal touchstone for the chronically online; with a bizarre mix of incisive, leftist political commentary, rants about Shadow the Hedgehog's merits as a potential partner, and a veneer of irony so thick it makes it impossible to tell what is truly sincere anymore, there's something for any of you little freaks out there.

Thanks for taking the time to look at my site and I hope to work with you soon :3
